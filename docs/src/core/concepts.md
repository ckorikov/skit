# Concepts

The entities `skit` operates on.

- **Curriculum** — a learning program in the broad sense. In `skit`, it
  is a repository of topics, modules, and study plans.
- **Syllabus** — a course plan: an ordered list of modules and topics
  tied to a study sequence and schedule.
- **Module** — a self-contained block of several topics united by a
  common learning goal.
- **Topic** — the minimal (atomic) unit of content; it belongs to a
  module or directly to a study plan.
- **Learning Outcome** — a statement of what a learner should be able to
  do after mastering a topic or module. In `skit` it is defined
  implicitly through a link to Evidence.
- **Evidence** — an artifact or work product (a test, a solution, a
  project) that confirms a learning outcome has been achieved.
